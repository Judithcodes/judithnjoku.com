About Me

My name is Judith and I am a writer and developer living in Nigeria. I created this website, in order to help document and share my learning experience with programming and other things that may interest me, hence I would be sharing stories, opinions, ideas, and lessons learned.

I am a graduate of Petroleum Engineering with lots of interests. I have taken up the 100days of code challenge to help me to study with discipline. Eventhough I work with a bank from 7am to 7pm daily, this challenge has enabled me to try to sacrifice at least one hour of my day for coding.

I mostly study from 2am to 4am every morning. I seem to be the most productive at such hours.

My goal is to learn to create revolutionary projects that will benefit and solve the problems of millions of persons across the world.

My idea of the perfect job is one that allows me to travel around the world, learn new things and write about it. Perhaps when I can, I will also share some of my adventures.

I also enjoy learning foreign languages. I actually have a blog i abandoned for quite some time: 9JAKOREAN. On this blog, I tried to teach others to learn korean the Nigerian way.I made youtube videos of my tutorials and shared them on the blog. In a way, trying to teach what I have learned, helps me to understand better. My love for that awesome language is unimaginable.

Besides coding, travelling and learning, I totally enjoy writing; creating stories, research and sharing my thoughts through articles. I also love trying out new recipes.

I would be sharing some of the stories I have written, although not completed. Maybe when I can, I will start a 100DaysOfWriting challenge, to propel me to finish what I have started.

I am the happiest when I am creating things.

Join me on this learning journey.

Cheers
